Vitæ

I'm a fifth year PhD student studying Computer Science at the University of Notre Dame advised by Tim Weninger. My research is primarily on graph grammars and graph neural networks, particularly on the formal and practical relationship between these two models. I also have tangential interests in category theory, logic, and philosophy of mathematics. In my spare time, I like to play music and boulder. I am bilingual in English and Spanish. I was born in La Habana, Cuba.
