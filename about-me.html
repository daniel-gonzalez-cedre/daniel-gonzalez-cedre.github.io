Vitæ

I'm a sixth year PhD student in Computer Science of at the University of Notre Dame advised by Tim Weninger. My research is primarily focussed on explainable machine learning and the relationship between graph grammars and graph neural networks. I'm also tangentially interested in category theory, logic, and their relationship with computing and graph theory. In my spare time, I like to play music and boulder. I'm bilingual in English and Spanish. I was born in the city of Havana, Cuba.
