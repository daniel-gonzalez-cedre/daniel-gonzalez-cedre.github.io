PhD Student · Computer Science @ the University of Notre Dame
Advisor : Dr. Tim Weninger
Artificial Intelligence Lab

My research is currently focused on finding a formalism for modeling the evolution of graphs over time and expressing that temporal behavior in an intelligible way. More broadly, my interests also include graph grammars, graph summarization, and graph generators. For fun, I like to study set theory, type theory, and foreign languages. For advanced fun, I like to boulder and play music.
