About Me
I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Notre Dame advised by Prof. Tim Weninger.
My research is primarily motivated by the desire to understand how graphs evolve over time. To that end, I focus on interpretable models for dynamic networks.
I am also interested in how users interact with and influence each other on social networks, especially when misinformation and disinformation are involved.

Currently, I am interning as a researcher at the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.
